Information Security Awareness for New Intern​

Interns are required to read and sign this Information Security Awareness to confirm that you have completed the new employee information security training program.
​ This Information Security Awareness for New Intern is only available for browsing and signing by the C02 personnel category. Other personnel categories cannot browse this page.

I、Computer and Network Usage Guidelines​ 

(Ⅰ)Computer and Network Usage Guidelines of ITRI​

  • You must install security software as specified, including ForeScout agent, HCL bigfix agent and TrendMicro Apex One.​
  • You can only use legally authorized software and must sign the "Commitment to use of lawful computer Software"
  • You cannot add unauthorized network devices. Computers connected to the ITRI's computer network cannot be connected to external networks and information devices through data terminals, data lines, or other means.​
  • If you violate laws or the Computer and Network Usage Guidelines of ITRI, you will be responsible for legal and compensation liabilities.
  • The reporting window of information security incidents : 18899. If you have any suspected or reported security incidents, you should report them immediately.

Computer Security Usage Behavior​

  • According to the regulations of ITRI, install specific security and protective software.​
  • Properly manage passwords and comply with the password security regulations of our institute.​ Go to the " ITRI Staffs Password Modification System "

Software usage Behavior​

  • Use licensed software and avoid using software from unknown sources to prevent malicious programs and unauthorized information leaks.

Internet usage behavior​

  • Comply with the basic principles of Internet use.​
  • Not allowed to cross-over external networks:Computers that have been connected to the ITRI's network cannot be connected to other external networks and information equipment through modems, data lines or other means simultaneously.
  • No Sniffing:It's not allowed to use interception technology to capture other people’s passwords or network transmission information. ​
  • No Scanning: It's not allowed to use any methods to collect information about the website, host, network, information security protection of ITRI or other organizations.​
  • Do not transmit or post materials that violate public order or good morals.​
  • Do not modify IP & Mac addresses of information equipment.​
  • Do not circumvent examinations of usage record.​

(II) Remote Work Notice​

  • Before starting remote work, please complete the Remote Work Training (Online) first.
  • When working remotely, please follow these guidelines:
    1. Use a computer dedicated to work and do not share it with family members.
    2. Avoid using public networks or public Wi-Fi connections to log in to the ITRI system. If necessary, use a personal mobile phone hotspot to connect.
    3. Use the provided work equipment to handle work-related information only.
    4. Install legal software and use it within the authorized scope.
    5. Do not use the same password as your ITRI account for systems not controlled by ITRI (such as Gmail).
    6. Keep your operating system and applications updated at all times.
    7. Be aware of social engineering email attacks. If you have any questions, seek assistance from 18899.

Ⅱ、Confidential Information Management

(Ⅰ) Confidential Information Scope

  • Confidential information refers to valuable information generated due to technology research and development, promotional services or business management, which disclose improperly will cause loss of assets, loss of competitiveness or harm to ITRI.
  • According to Article 5 of the Confidential Information Management Regulations of ITRI, the scope of confidential information as follows:
    1. Intellectual property created by ITRI's research and development, such as: unpublished patents, unpublished research papers, research record books, research reports, and other technical data.
    2. Customer information and confidential information requested by customers, such as: documents exchanged by the confidentiality contract.
    3. Contracts and plans for execution, such as: cooperation agreements, plans, and cost baselines.
    4. Personal related information, such as: personal information, salary, performance evaluation, etc.
    5. Files related to major customer disputes and litigation currently being handled by the ITRI.
    6. Information that must be kept confidential due to the nature of each business.
    7. Scope of confidential information for overall business and management of the ITRI.
    8. Other information that must be kept confidential to protect the rights and interests of ITRI.

(Ⅳ) Principles and responsibilities for the safekeeping of confidential information

  • Responsibility for the custody of confidential information: Documents that are being written or have not yet been approved shall be kept by individuals. Confidential documents after registration shall be handed over to confidential document control personnel for safekeeping.
  • Consequences of leaking confidential information: The ITRI employment contract has stipulated the obligation to keep confidentiality and shall not leak it. Violators shall bear civil, criminal and special legal liability. If there is a leak due to your own fault or if you know that someone else has leaked it, you should report it immediately.

Mark

The marking contains Confidentiality Level and Confidentiality Statement in both Chinese and English.

Register

"Confidential" and "Strictly Confidential" documents should be registered and kept by document control personnel.​

Safekeeping

Documents should be locked  or have access control, and files should be password-protected  or storage device password-protected or access rights controlled.​

Circulation control​

It needs to apply for documents circulation. It is not allowed to circulate to unauthorized persons for use. 

Delivery method

Delivery method:​
  • Confidential documents should be sealed in a confidential bag and delivered by registered mail or by hand. Encryption is required when sending via email.
  • Official documents should be transmitted usinghe electronic communication system provided by ITRI. . It is strictly prohibited to use non-ITRI information systems such as Line for transmission.​
  •   "Strictly Confidential" documents cannot be transmitted using electronic communication systems such as email.​
